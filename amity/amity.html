Amity

Social network and real time chat platform

Details:

Worked with another android developer on a complex social networking app with a featureset that included: Real time chat, real time updates to content (like counts, user info etc), posting and displaying various types of content (Video, images, gifs), high performance scrolling through lists of heavy content, reactions to content, push notifications, and many more.

Technologies

  • Kotlin
  • MVVM
  • RxJava
  • GRPC & Protobuf
  • Clean Architecture
  • Dagger
  • Firebase
  • Google Fit
