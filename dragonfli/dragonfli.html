Dragonfli

Branded 'channels' and music streaming service

Details:

Dragonfli was an app I worked on after Guvera, it was a pivot from the guvera product but mostly rewritten from the ground up.
It contained music streaming functionality like Guvera but was focused on branded "channels". It included more video content, native article functionality and beacons support. I worked on the beacon functionality that made use of the phone's Bluetooth to communicate with other BLE devices.
We also made an effort to take more advantage of RxJava's functionality for this codebase.
